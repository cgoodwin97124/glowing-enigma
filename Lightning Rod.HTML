BACKGROUND
Jesse Fader served in the US Army as an infantryman and MP. After discharge, he drifted for a bit. During a superhero fight, he found himself engaging in crowd control, using his skills from the military; the fight was against a master villain type named Dyaeus Phater, who claimed to be an ancient storm god. The fight resulted in one of Phater's artifacts being shattered; Jesse came into contact with the energies and some shards from the shattered artifact, and found himself changed.
